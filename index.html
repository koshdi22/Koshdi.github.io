Terms of Service

Payment & Deposits +

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your commission slot. Final payment is due upon completion before delivery. I accept payment through PayPal, Stripe, and bank transfer.

Usage Rights +

Upon full payment, you receive personal use rights. Commercial use, merchandise resale, or reproduction requires written permission and additional licensing fees. All artwork remains my intellectual property unless otherwise agreed.

Revisions & Deliverables +

The number of revisions is tier-dependent and applies to major changes only. Minor tweaks are included. Revisions must be requested within 7 days of first draft. Additional revisions beyond the limit will incur a fee of $25 each.

Cancellation Policy +

If you cancel before work begins, your deposit is non-refundable but can be applied to a future commission. Cancellations after work has started will result in no refund. Refunds for my cancellation (rare) are issued in full within 14 days.

Timeline +

Delivery times are estimates. Rush fees of 50% apply for expedited delivery. I cannot guarantee delivery during personal emergencies or health issues. Final artwork will be delivered as a digital file (PNG/PSD/PDF as agreed).

Credit & Sharing +

I reserve the right to share your commissioned work on my portfolio and social media with proper credit. If you wish for your commission to remain private, this must be agreed upon beforehand at no additional cost.

About

she / her — 🇺🇦 Ukrainian-based artist

KOSHDI is a digital 2D artist specializing in black-and-white portraiture and celebrity illustration. Working at the intersection of pop culture and fine art, my work explores fame, identity, and the mythology we build around public figures. Each piece captures not just likeness but essence — the quality that makes someone unforgettable.

With a style rooted in high-contrast ink techniques translated to digital, I create intimate portraits of celebrities both known and emerging. I believe in celebrating the human beneath the fame.
